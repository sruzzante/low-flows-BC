Groundwater Wells Spatial view intended to show location and other attributes of wells which have been drilled, dug or driven in the province, to assist in the management and protection of the groundwater resource. Point features showing the location of groundwater wells in BC joined with attributes and information from the Groundwater Wells and Aquifers (GWELLS) application. Column Details
Column Name Short Name Data Type Data Precision Column Comments
GW_WW_SYSID SYSID NUMBER 10 A system generated unique identification number
WELL_TAG_NUMBER WELL_TAG NUMBER 10 The file number assigned to a particular well in the in the provincial Groundwater Wells and Aquifers application, e.g., 56809.
IDENTIFICATION_PLATE_NUMBER ID_PLATE NUMBER 10 Steel plate with a unique number that is attached to required wells under the groundwater protection regulations such as water supply wells, recharge or injection wells made by drilling or boring, and permanent dewatering wells, e.g., 691.
STREET_ADDRESS ADDRESS VARCHAR2 200 Street address of the property that the well is physically located on.
CITY CITY VARCHAR2 50 The city, town, or general locality where the well is located, e.g. Oliver, Gabriola Island.
LEGAL_LOT LEGAL_LOT VARCHAR2 10 The smallest designation of land (the lot number) from the legal description of the land parcel where the well is located, e.g., B, 2.
LEGAL_PLAN LEGAL_PLAN VARCHAR2 20 The plan number from the legal description of the land parcel where the well is located, e.g., 1261, 13604, LMP7728.
LEGAL_DISTRICT_LOT LGL_DSTRCT VARCHAR2 20 The district lot number from the legal description of the land parcel where the well is located, e.g., 456, 1234s.
LEGAL_PID LEGAL_PID NUMBER 10 The parcel identifier (PID) of the land parcel where the well is located, e.g., 014905736.
WELL_LOCATION_DESCRIPTION LOCN_DESCR VARCHAR2 500 An informal description of where the well is located on a property, e.g., 50m to the right of the house and behind the shed.
COORDINATE_ACQUISITION_CODE COORDAQ VARCHAR2 1 The approximate accuracy of the geographic coordinates provided, e.g., H (default accuracy; (10 m accuracy) handheld GPS with accuracy of +/- 10 meters), A (10 m accuracy - cadastre and good location sketch), C (50 m accuracy - Digitized from 1:20,000 mapping), I (20 m accuracy - no cadastre but good location sketch or good written description), G (unknown, accuracy based on parcel size - No cadastre, poor or no location sketch; site located in center of primary parcel), J (unknown, accuracy based on parcel size - cadastre, poor or no location sketch, arbitrarily located in center of parcel).
WELL_STATUS WELLSTATUS VARCHAR2 300 Description of the status of a well as defined in the Groundwater Protection Regulation of the Water Act, i.e. New, Abandoned, Alteration, Closure, Other.
WELL_CLASS WELL_CLASS VARCHAR2 100 Valid classification as defined in the Groundwater Protection Regulation of the Water Sustainability Act, i.e., Water Supply, Monitoring, Recharge, Injection, Dewatering, Drainage, Remediation, Geotechnical, Closed-loop geoexchange.
WELL_SUBCLASS WLL_SBCLSS VARCHAR2 20 Valid subclassification as defined in the Groundwater Protection Regulation of the Water Sustainability Act, e.g., Temporary, Permanent, Borehole, Artesian.
INTENDED_WATER_USE INTEND_USE VARCHAR2 100 The intended use of the water in a water supply well as reported by the driller at time of work completion on the well, e.g., private domestic, irrigation, water supply system, industrial commercial, unknown.
WELL_DETAILS_URL WELL_URL VARCHAR2 255 Link to the well summary report within the Groundwater Wells and Aquifer (GWELLS) application, which provides the overall description and history of the well.
LICENCE_STATUS LIC_STATUS VARCHAR2 300 Description of valid licencing option granted to a well under the Water Water Sustainability Act. This information comes from eLicensing, i.e. Unlicensed, Licensed, Historical
AQUIFER_ID AQUIFER_ID NUMBER 10 System generated unique sequential number assigned to each mapped aquifer. The aquifer ID identifies which aquifer a well is in. An aquifer can have multiple wells, while a single well can only be in one aquifer.
PERSON_RESPONSIBLE PERSON VARCHAR2 300 Name (and registration ID) of the registered driller or pump installer responsible for constructing, altering, or decommissioning the well, e.g., Jane Doe (WD 06666666).
COMPANY_OF_PERSON_RESPONSIBLE COMPANY VARCHAR2 300 Name (and location) of the registered driller or pump installer company responsible for constructing, altering, or decommissioning the well, e.g., Geotech Drilling Services Ltd. (Vernon, BC).
FINISHED_WELL_DEPTH FNSH_DEPTH NUMBER 8 The depth at which the well was finished, measured in units of feet bgl (below ground level), e.g. 157.0. It can be shallower than the total well depth which is the total depth at which the well was drilled.
TOTAL_DEPTH_DRILLED TTL_DEPTH NUMBER 7 The deepest depth a well was drilled to, reported as feet below ground level, e.g., 150.
BEDROCK_DEPTH BED_DEPTH NUMBER 8 Depth below ground level at which bedrock starts, measured in feet, e.g. 132.0.
YIELD YIELD NUMBER 8 An approximate estimate of the capacity of the well to produce groundwater. Estimated by the well driller during construction by conducting a well yield test. Measured in US Gallons/minute.
YIELD_ESTIMATION_DURATION YIELD_DUR NUMBER 9 Length of time (in hours) over which a yield test was conducted by the driller during construction to determine well yield, e.g., 3.25.
ARTESIAN_COND ARTSN_CND CHAR 1 Indicates whether artesian conditions are present for a given well, i.e., Y, N.
ARTESIAN_FLOW ARTSN_FLW NUMBER 8 Measurement of the artesian well water flow that occurs naturally due to inherent water pressure in the well. Pressure within the aquifer forces the groundwater to rise above the land surface naturally without using a pump. Flowing artesian wells can flow on an intermittent or continuous basis. Measured in US Gallons per minute.
ARTESIAN_PRESSURE ARTSN_PRSS NUMBER 8 Pressure of the water coming out of an artesian well as measured at the time of well construction, measured in PSI (pounds per square inch).
STATIC_WATER_LEVEL STATIC_LVL NUMBER 8 The level (depth below ground) to which water will naturally rise in a well without pumping, measured in feet, e.g. 280.0
DIAMETER DIAMETER NUMBER 8 The diameter of the casing of the well, measured in inches, e.g. 6.62. There can be multiple casings in a well, e.g. surface casing, and production casing. Diameter of casing made available to the public is generally the production casing.
FINAL_CASING_STICK_UP STICKUP NUMBER 6 Tthe length of the production casing in the well that is above either (a) the surface of the ground adjacent to the well, or (b) the floor of the well sump, pump house or well pit. Measured as inches, e.g., 30.
GROUND_ELEVATION GRND_ELEV NUMBER 10 The elevation of the well at ground level. Measured as feet above sea level, e.g., 1050
GROUND_ELEVATION_METHOD GRND_METHD VARCHAR2 10 Description of how ground elevation of the well was measured, e.g., unknown, GPS, level, 20K_map, 5K_map, DIFF_GPS, altimeter, 10K_map, 50K_map.
SURFACE_SEAL_DEPTH SEAL_DEPTH NUMBER 5 The total depth (length) of the protective surface seal installed around the outside of the outermost casing or between multiple casings, measured in feet, e.g. 16.
SURFACE_SEAL_THICKNESS SEAL_THICK NUMBER 7 The distance between the outside of the outermost casing and natural ground, or the distance between multiple casings in which the sealant is installed. Measured in inches, e.g., 2.
SURFACE_SEAL_METHOD SRFC_SL_MD VARCHAR2 10 Method of how the surface seal was installed around the well casing, i.e., pumped, poured, other.
SCREEN_INTAKE_METHOD SCREENMTHD VARCHAR2 10 Main entry for water into the well, i.e., open bottom, uncased hole, and screen.
WATER_SUPPLY_SYSTEM_NAME SYSTM_NAME VARCHAR2 80 Owner of the water supply system using the well, e.g., Township of Langley, Cedar Lake Water System, Copperside Estates.
WATER_SUPPLY_SYSTEM_WELL_NAME SYSTM_WELL VARCHAR2 80 Well name or identifier used by the water supply system, e.g., Well#5, north well, pine street well.
CONSTRUCTION_START_DATE CNSTRCT_ST DATE 7 The date that the well construction was started.
CONSTRUCTION_END_DATE CNSTRCT_EN DATE 7 The date that the well construction was completed.
ALTERATION_START_DATE ALTER_ST DATE 7 The date that alteration works, such as increasing the well depth or diameter, were started.
ALTERATION_END_DATE ALTER_EN DATE 7 The date that alteration works, such as increasing the well depth or diameter, were completed.
DECOMMISSION_START_DATE DECOM_ST DATE 7 The date that closure works were started in order to take a well permanently out of service.
DECOMMISSION_END_DATE DECOM_EN DATE 7 The date that closure works were completed in order to take a well permanently out of service.
DECOMMISSION_REASON DECOM_REAS VARCHAR2 250 Description or rationale for why well was closed permanently, e.g., dry hole, no longer in use, contamination risk.
OBSERVATION_WELL_NUMBER OBSWELLNUM VARCHAR2 30 The number assigned to a well within the Provincial Groundwater Observation Well Network, e.g., 552.
OBSERVATION_WELL_STATUS OBSWELLSTS VARCHAR2 10 The current status of a well within the Provincial Groundwater Observation Well Network, e.g., Active, Inactive.
EMS_ID EMS_ID VARCHAR2 30 A unique identifier from the provincial Environmental Monitoring System (EMS), to be used for connecting to any well monitoring data, e.g., E218071, 1401072.
AQUIFER_MATERIAL AQFR_MTRL VARCHAR2 100 A high-level description of the aquifer material type the well is located within, i.e., bedrock, unconsolidated, or unknown.

SE_ANNO_CAD_DATA BLOB 4000 A binary column used by spatial tools to store annotation, curve features and CAD data when using the SDO_GEOMETRY storage data type.
